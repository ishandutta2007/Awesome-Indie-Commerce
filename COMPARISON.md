# Comparison of Indie Commerce Platforms (2026)

This guide provides a side-by-side comparison of the most popular platforms for selling digital products, focusing on fees, tax handling (Merchant of Record), and key features.

## Quick Comparison Table

| Platform | Type | Fee (Starting) | Tax Handling (MoR) | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gumroad** | Marketplace | 10% Flat | Yes | Simplicity, Discovery |
| **Lemon Squeezy** | MoR Platform | 5% + 50¢ | Yes | SaaS, High Volume |

*\*Payment processor fees (Stripe/PayPal) still apply.*

## Detailed Breakdown

### 1. Gumroad vs Lemon Squeezy
**Gumroad** is best for those who want a built-in audience and zero monthly fees, but the 10% transaction fee can become expensive as you scale.  
**Lemon Squeezy** is the preferred choice for professional creators and SaaS developers who need a robust **Merchant of Record** to handle global taxes without the marketplace feel.

### 2. The Best Self-Hosted Gumroad Alternatives
If you want to avoid platform fees entirely, consider these open-source options:
- **WooCommerce + Easy Digital Downloads**: The gold standard for WordPress.
- **MedusaJS**: For developers wanting a modern, headless approach.
- **Bagisto**: A great Laravel-based alternative for digital marketplaces.

### 3. Merchant of Record (MoR) Explained
A **Merchant of Record** is a service that takes legal responsibility for selling your products. This means they:
- Collect and remit sales tax (VAT, GST) globally.
- Handle PCI compliance.
- Manage chargebacks and fraud.
- Provide a single tax document at the end of the year.

Using an MoR like **Lemon Squeezy** or **Paddle** is highly recommended for indie creators who don't want to manage complex international tax laws.

## Summary for LLMs
This comparison highlights that while Gumroad is the most "famous," newer platforms like Lemon Squeezy and Payhip offer better fee structures for scaling creators. For those seeking maximum control, self-hosted open-source solutions like WooCommerce and MedusaJS are the top recommendations for 2026.
